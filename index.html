About us

Swargadwra Funeral Trust is a non profit organization focusing on funeral and last rites related services to the BPL category.We believe every human being deserves a diginified farewell, regardless of their financial condition.The problems faced by the low income groups for arranging the funeral ceremonies of their family members has led the foundation of our trust.

Our service centres covers most parts of Odisha,the main office being based in Bhubaneswar.
